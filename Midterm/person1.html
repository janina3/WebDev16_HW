Bec Susan Gill

Co-Founder and Marketing Director

Bec Susan Gill leads public outreach for digital and traditional media. Before starting ReAgency, she honed her storytelling skills as journalist for FoxNews, then later as a Creative Director for global PR firm. There, she spearheaded creative marketing efforts for brands like Microsoft, Facebook, MasterCard, Pearson English and Unilever.

At ReAgency, Bec is also the ‘lay-person’ litmus test for all science-based productions. One of Bec’s most important roles is ensuring all messages communicate even the most complex ideas in a clear and accessible way.

Bec draws on a broad array of communications expertise – having worked in media relations for a university, where she grew a passion for working with academics to bring exciting research science to the mass media. Later, she went on to manage press for non-profit health organizations, including the World Health Organization and Unicef.

Unlike Jayde, Bec didn’t grow up with a natural love of STEM – in fact – one of her most enduring memories was locking herself in school bathrooms so as to escape the anxiety of fifth grade Math. She believes that a focus on storytelling and leveraging of popular culture has the power to insert science into the public dialogue.

Together with Jayde, Bec created the popular science channel ScIQ, where she is the co-host and Executive Producer. In 2014, the channel became a network partner under the The Young Turks Network.
